A servant, Disciple, Follower, Evagelist, Revilvalist, Father and Teacher of God's Word - Evangelist Reinhard Bonnke

About the Legend

The death of evangelist and missionary Reinhard Bonnke has created an outpouring of touching tributes from around the word. The founder of Christ for all Nations(CfaN) passed away on Dec.7 at the age of 79.

Evangelist Franklin Graham said,

"Reinhard Bonnke was a powerful evangelist around the world, but particularly on the continent of Africa where his heart burned with compassion for people's soul. Throught the preaching of the Gospel of the Lord Jesus Christ, millions of lives will be i heaven and we give praise and glory to God that His Word never return void. Our thoughts and prayers are with Reinhard's wife Anni and the entire family."

After graduating from the Bible College of Wales and returning to Germany, Bonnke led a series of meetings in Rendsburg. He began receiving speaking invitations from all around Germanyand the rest the world.
